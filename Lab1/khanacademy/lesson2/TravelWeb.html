Basic info about Hawaii

Hawaii is a group of islands in the Pacific Ocean and the 50th state of the USA. It is famous for its beautiful beaches, volcanoes, and warm tropical climate. Hawaii is a popular destination for tourism, surfing, and nature lovers.

Local Hawaiian Food

  • Poke Bowl — fresh raw fish served with rice, seaweed, and local sauces.
  • Loco Moco — rice topped with a beef patty, fried egg, and rich gravy.
  • Kalua Pork — slow-cooked pork with a smoky flavor, traditional Hawaiian dish.
